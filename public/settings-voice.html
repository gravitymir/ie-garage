Voice input

Provider

Uses the browser's built-in Web Speech API. Works in Chrome, Edge and Safari on desktop and Android. No account, no key, no monthly bill. Firefox needs media.webspeech.recognition.enable in about:config to be turned on.

Recognition language

Applies to every mic button in the app — Notes on the job page, and any future voice input elsewhere. Change and Save; the next time you tap a mic icon it uses the new language.

Test the microphone

Tap the button, allow microphone access, speak a short phrase, tap again to stop. Confirms the browser + language combo works before you rely on it in a job.
