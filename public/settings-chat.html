Chat notifications

Events posted to the chat feed

Turn on the workshop events you want the shared chat to show. Turning an event off just hides it from the feed — the underlying data (jobs, stock counts) is unaffected.

Feed depth & cleanup

Pick how far back the chat feed should show. Any combination of months + days + hours adds up — e.g. 2 months, 15 days, or just 12 hours. Leave all zero to keep the feed unlimited.

Close chat when idle

Chat window slides itself away after this many minutes of no activity inside it (mouse / keys / new messages). Only affects when the chat is open — a closed chat costs nothing anyway.
